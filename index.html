Attend to Earn

  • Fan Loyalty and Attendance

  • Events ly enhances fan loyalty and attendance through the Attend-to-Earn model. This model incentivises event attendance by rewarding users with loyalty points and a ‘digital moment’ collectible.

Collectable Content

  • Coupled with tickets, such as art, music, and video, allow fans to enjoy or resell.

Resale royalties

  • Enable ticket issuers to receive a payment on every resale.

Blochain Ticketing

  • Verifiable,On-chain Tickets that are 100% Secure & Private while also being completely fraud & counterfeit proof.

Rotating QR codes

  • Ensures that only redeemed tickets are validated, and the ticket holder is the one redeeming.
